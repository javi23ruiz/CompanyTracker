Welcome to the Company Tracker


The news are retrieved from the NewsApi developed by Google. Please enter a company name and an email address. After the appplication is executed, an email will be sent and a podcast will be generated to listen to the summary of the news.

  • News are retrieved from Google News API and thery are filtered out by relevancy.
  • By default the application returns the 10 most important news from the last two weeks.
  • Enter a valid email address and an email with the summary of the news article and the links will be sent to you from tracker.news.web@gmail.com.
  • Enter the company name you would like to be updated with.
